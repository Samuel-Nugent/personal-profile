About Me

I’m a passionate front-end developer with a love for clean code and intuitive design. I specialize in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, focusing on building fast, accessible, and responsive web applications. I’m just about to venture into Vue and Angular as I haven’t had chance to use them yet.

Over the past year, I progressed through web development bootcamp where I learnt much about the basics of front end (HTML and CSS) all the way to the complexity of server side back end databases (NoSQL - MongoDB). I’ve worked on a range of projects—from landing pages to complex dashboards—always with a mindset of continuous improvement and user-first design. I enjoy collaborating with designers and backend developers to bring ideas to life.

While I’ve explored backend development, my focus remains on the frontend and whilst I see myself eventually becoming a full-stack developer, for the time being I would consider myself a front-end developer.

I’ve worked with Git and GitHub. I'm also learning C#, PHP, Figma, and Webpack. I’m always eager to explore new tech and refine my current skillset. Whether it's optimizing performance or enhancing accessibility, I enjoy crafting seamless user experiences.

When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me learning something new or sketching interface ideas. I'm currently open to new opportunities and always excited to collaborate on meaningful projects.
