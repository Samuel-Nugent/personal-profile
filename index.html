About Me

I’m a passionate front-end developer with a love for clean code and intuitive design. I specialise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. My focus is on building fast, accessible, and responsive web applications. I’ve just started to venture into Vue and Angular.

Over the past year, I progressed through web developer bootcamp where I learnt full stack development. I’ve worked on a range of projects, from landing pages to complex dashboards, always with a mindset of continuous improvement and user-first design.

I’m currently learning C#, Angular and Vue. my next aim is to gain mastery of Wordpress, PHP and Figma to become a fully-versed front-end developer. I’m always eager to explore new tech and refine my current skillset. Whether it's optimisng performance or enhancing accessibility, I enjoy crafting seamless user experiences.

When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me learning something new or sketching interface ideas. I'm currently open to new opportunities and always excited to collaborate on meaningful projects.
